Notes:

  • Use the "New Grid" button to clear the space and change grid size if you want.
  • Switch between rainbow and black by hitting the "Color" button.
  • Turn gradually increasing opacity on or off with the "Opacity" button.
  • *Both rainbow and gradually increasing opacity are turned on by default.
  • *You can right click and left click somewhere else in the canvas if you want to "pick up your pencil".
